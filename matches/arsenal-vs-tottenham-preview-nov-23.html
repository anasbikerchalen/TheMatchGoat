Arsenal vs. Tottenham Hotspur: North London Derby Preview, Prediction & Betting Tips

The Premier League weekend reaches a fever pitch with the biggest rivalry in English football: the North London Derby. Arsenal welcome neighbors Tottenham Hotspur to the Emirates Stadium for a clash that guarantees passion, drama, and goals.

Mikel Arteta’s Gunners are chasing the title and view this as a must-win game to keep pace at the top. Meanwhile, Ange Postecoglou’s Spurs are fighting for Champions League qualification and would love nothing more than to derail their rivals' title hopes in their own backyard.

Can Arsenal’s disciplined structure contain "Ange-ball," or will Spurs’ high-risk, high-reward style cause chaos at the Emirates? Here is your ultimate guide to the match.

Match Details

  • Fixture: Arsenal vs. Tottenham Hotspur
  • Date: Thursday, November 20, 2025
  • Kick-Off: 2:00 PM GMT (UK) / 9:00 AM ET (USA)
  • Venue: Emirates Stadium, London
  • Competition: Premier League

Team News & Updates

Arsenal

The Gunners have received a massive boost with Bukayo Saka fully fit and ready to terrorize the wings. Captain Martin Ødegaard is expected to pull the strings in midfield. The main decision for Arteta comes at left-back, where he must choose between the defensive solidity of Jurriën Timber or the inversion of Riccardo Calafiori to combat Spurs' pace. William Saliba and Gabriel will anchor the defense.

Tottenham Hotspur

Spurs arrive with their typical all-out attack philosophy. Captain Son Heung-min loves scoring in this fixture and will lead the line or drift inside from the left. James Maddison is the key creator, but concerns remain over the fitness of Micky van de Ven. If the speedy center-back misses out, Spurs' high defensive line could be extremely vulnerable to Arsenal's balls over the top.

Prediction & Head-to-Head

The North London Derby rarely disappoints. Historically, home advantage is huge in this fixture; Arsenal has dominated Spurs at the Emirates in recent years.

Tactically, this sets up perfectly for Arsenal. Spurs will aggressively press and hold a high line, which plays into the hands of Saka and Gabriel Martinelli in transition. Arsenal is arguably the best team in the league at set-pieces, which is a known weakness for Tottenham. While Spurs will likely score given their attacking quality, Arsenal’s defensive structure is superior.

Score Prediction: Arsenal 3 – 1 Tottenham Hotspur
