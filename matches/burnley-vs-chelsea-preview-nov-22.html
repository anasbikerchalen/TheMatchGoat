Burnley vs. Chelsea: Preview, Predictions & Betting Tips (Nov 22)

The Premier League returns to action this Saturday as Chelsea travel to Turf Moor to face Burnley in the early kick-off.

The Blues are looking to extend their winning run and solidify their place in the top four, while the Clarets are desperate for points to climb away from the relegation zone. It is set to be a battle of possession versus grit in Lancashire.

Here is your complete guide to the match, including team news, expert predictions, and the best betting tips for the weekend.

Match Details

  • Fixture: Burnley vs. Chelsea
  • Date: Thursday, November 20, 2025
  • Kick-Off: 12:30 PM GMT (UK) / 7:30 AM ET (USA)
  • Venue: Turf Moor
  • Competition: Premier League

Team News & Updates

Chelsea

Enzo Maresca’s side has received positive news following the international break. Cole Palmer and Reece James have both returned to training and are expected to be available for selection. However, the Blues will be without Wesley Fofana, who is serving a one-match suspension due to yellow card accumulation. Nicolas Jackson is expected to lead the line, supported by the creativity of Palmer and Pedro Neto.

Burnley

Scott Parker’s men are facing a defensive injury crisis. Key center-backs remain sidelined, meaning Burnley will likely field a makeshift defense against one of the league's most potent attacks. The hosts will rely heavily on Josh Brownhill in midfield and the pace of Luca Koleosho to catch Chelsea on the counter-attack.

Prediction & Head-to-Head

Chelsea boasts an impressive record at Turf Moor, remaining unbeaten in their last several visits. The tactical matchup favors the visitors; Burnley’s attempt to play out from the back often leaves them vulnerable to high-pressing teams like Chelsea.

While the atmosphere at Turf Moor is always hostile for the early kick-off, Chelsea’s individual quality should be the difference-maker. Expect the Blues to control possession and create plenty of chances.

Score Prediction: Burnley 0 - 3 Chelsea
