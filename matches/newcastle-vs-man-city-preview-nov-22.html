Newcastle vs. Manchester City: Prediction, Betting Tips & Where to Watch (Nov 22)

The highlight of the Premier League weekend takes place at St. James' Park this Saturday evening as Newcastle United host the reigning champions Manchester City.

Eddie Howe’s Magpies have turned their home ground into a cauldron of noise, making this one of the toughest away days in the calendar for Pep Guardiola. With City aiming to keep their title defense on track and Newcastle fighting to break back into the European spots, the stakes couldn't be higher.

Will the champions roll on, or can the Toon Army inspire a famous upset under the lights? Here is your complete preview, including team news, predictions, and betting tips.

Match Details

  • Fixture: Newcastle United vs. Manchester City
  • Date: Saturday, November 22
  • Kick-Off: 5:30 PM GMT (UK) / 12:30 PM ET (USA)
  • Venue: St. James' Park, Newcastle
  • Competition: Premier League

Team News & Updates

Newcastle United

Newcastle will need to be at their physical best to disrupt City’s rhythm. Alexander Isak is fully fit and expected to lead the line, looking to exploit the space behind City’s high line. Anthony Gordon offers a massive threat with his pace on the wing. However, the Magpies are still managing long-term absentees in defense, meaning Dan Burn and Fabian Schär will have their hands full against Erling Haaland.

Manchester City

Pep Guardiola faces a quick turnaround after the international break. Kevin De Bruyne is being managed carefully but is expected to play a part in such a high-profile fixture. All eyes will be on Erling Haaland, the league's top scorer, who will be looking to add to his tally. Rodri remains the heartbeat of the midfield; City’s win rate drops significantly without him, so his presence in the starting XI is vital.

Prediction & Head-to-Head

Recent meetings between these two at St. James' Park have been thrilling, high-scoring affairs (including a memorable 3-3 draw in recent seasons). Newcastle plays with high intensity at home, pressing aggressively, which can unsettle City.

However, Manchester City typically finds a way to grind out results in these difficult environments. While Newcastle will likely score given City’s tendency to concede on the counter-attack this season, Guardiola’s side possesses too much quality in the final third.

Score Prediction: Newcastle 1 – 2 Manchester City

  • Value Bet: Manchester City to Win & Over 2.5 Goals
