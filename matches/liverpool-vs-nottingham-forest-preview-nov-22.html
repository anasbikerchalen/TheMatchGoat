Liverpool vs. Nottingham Forest: Prediction, Betting Tips & Where to Watch (Nov 22)

The Premier League action continues this Saturday as Liverpool welcomes Nottingham Forest to Anfield.

Arne Slot’s Reds are looking to maintain their title charge and turn Anfield into an impenetrable fortress. However, they face a Nottingham Forest side that has proven to be a dangerous opponent on the road under Nuno Espírito Santo. Forest shocked the world with a win at this venue recently—can they strike lightning twice, or will Liverpool exact revenge with a dominant display?

Here is your complete guide to the match, including team news, predicted lineups, expert predictions, and the best betting tips.

Match Details

  • Fixture: Liverpool vs. Nottingham Forest
  • Date: Thursday, November 20, 2025
  • Kick-Off: 3:00 PM GMT (UK) / 10:00 AM ET (USA)
  • Venue: Anfield, Liverpool
  • Competition: Premier League Matchweek 12

Team News & Updates

Liverpool

Liverpool enters the weekend relatively healthy, though there are slight concerns over Alexis Mac Allister, who returned late from international duty in South America. If rested, expect Curtis Jones to slot into midfield. Mohamed Salah is in blistering form and will start on the right, while Diogo Jota is pushing to start ahead of Darwin Núñez as the central striker. Goalkeeper Alisson Becker is expected to start between the posts.

Nottingham Forest

Forest will be relying on their counter-attacking pace. Their key playmaker, Morgan Gibbs-White, is fit and will be the main creative outlet. Striker Chris Wood has been efficient in front of goal this season and will look to bully the Liverpool center-backs. In defense, Murillo and Nikola Milenković face a stern test against Liverpool’s fluid front three.

Prediction & Head-to-Head

History suggests this should be a home win, but Nottingham Forest has become a “bogey team” for big clubs due to their disciplined low block and rapid transitions. Liverpool will dominate possession (likely 65%+) and look to pin Forest back. The key battle will be on the wings; if Liverpool’s full-backs push too high, Forest’s Anthony Elanga and Callum Hudson-Odoi could exploit the space behind. However, Liverpool’s firepower at Anfield is usually overwhelming. Expect a tight first half followed by Liverpool breaking through in the second.

Score Prediction: Liverpool 3 – 1 Nottingham Forest

Best Betting Tips

  • Banker Bet: Liverpool to Win & Over 2.5 Goals
  • Player Prop: Mohamed Salah to Score Anytime
  • Value Pick: Both Teams to Score (BTTS) — Yes

  • United States: Peacock Premium (streaming exclusively)
  • Canada: FuboTV Canada
  • Australia: Optus Sport
  • International: Check local listings for beIN Sports or SuperSport depending on your region.
