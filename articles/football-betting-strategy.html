The best way to bet on football is Positive Expected Value (+EV) betting. This means placing bets where the probability of winning is higher than the odds imply. You achieve this by Line Shopping (comparing sportsbooks) and beating the Closing Line.

1. Stop Betting Teams, Start Betting Numbers

Casual bettors say, "The Chiefs are definitely going to win." Professional bettors say, "The Chiefs are -6.5. My model makes them -8.5. There is value in this number."

The single best way to win long-term is Line Shopping. If DraftKings has the Bills at -3 and FanDuel has them at -2.5, taking the -2.5 increases your win probability by roughly 4-5% over the course of a season. For a deeper dive, read how line shopping impacts long-term ROI.

Action Step: Never bet without checking at least 3 different sportsbooks.

2. Master Your Bankroll (The 1% Rule)

The fastest way to lose is to vary your bet size based on "confidence." Emotion is the enemy of profit. The best strategy is flat-betting or using a simplified version of the Kelly Criterion, a formula used by investors to balance risk and reward.

3. The "Hidden" Edge: Correlated Parlays

Most guides tell you parlays are "sucker bets." Generally, they are right. However, there is one exception: Correlation.

In football, events are connected. If a Quarterback throws for 300+ yards, it is highly likely his WR1 also has a big game.

  • Bad Parlay: Chiefs to Win + Lakers to Win (No correlation).
  • Good Strategy: Patrick Mahomes OVER 285.5 Yards + Travis Kelce OVER 70.5 Yards.

Sportsbooks often fail to price this correlation perfectly. This is one of the best ways to bet on player props in 2025.

4. Tracking Closing Line Value (CLV)

How do you know if you are a "sharp" bettor? You beat the Closing Line.

If you bet the Lions at +4 on Tuesday, and by kickoff on Sunday the line is Lions +1, you made a great bet, regardless of the game's outcome. You obtained 3 points of value for free. If you consistently beat the closing line, the math guarantees you will profit over time.

5. Micro-Betting: The New Frontier

Live betting is evolving. The new "best way" to leverage knowledge of game flow is Micro-Betting (betting on the result of the next drive).

If you see a defense is exhausted (hands on hips, slow substitutions), betting "Next Drive Result: Touchdown" allows you to capitalize on visual data before the algorithm adjusts.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the safest bet in football?

There is no "safe" bet in gambling, but the lowest variance bet is typically a straight Moneyline bet on a favorite or a Double Chance bet (Win or Draw) in soccer. However, safe bets usually have low payouts.

Is there a strategy to always win football bets?

The only way to guarantee a profit is Arbitrage Betting, where you bet on all possible outcomes across different sportsbooks with differing odds to lock in a profit regardless of the result.

What is the +EV betting strategy?

+EV (Positive Expected Value) means making a bet where the probability of winning is higher than the implied probability of the odds offered. Over time, this is the only mathematical way to beat the bookies.
