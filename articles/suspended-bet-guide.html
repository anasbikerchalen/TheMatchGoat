Why Games Get Suspended

Understanding why a game is halted is the first step in comprehending its impact on your bet. Common reasons include:

  • Weather: Rain, lightning, or extreme heat (common in Baseball & Football).
  • Safety: Player injuries or medical emergencies.
  • Technical: Floodlight failure or VAR issues.

The Golden Rule: Is Your Stake Safe?

The Short Answer:

In 90% of cases, if a game is suspended and not finished within 24-48 hours, your stake is returned (voided).

Frequently Asked Questions

What does "Void Bet" mean?

A void bet means the wager is nullified. It's treated as if it never happened, and your full stake is returned to your account balance.

What happens to my Parlay?

The suspended leg is removed. A 4-team parlay becomes a 3-team parlay. You don't lose the whole bet, but the potential payout decreases.

What about Live Bets?

If the outcome was already decided (e.g., "First Goal Scorer"), the bet stands. If the outcome was still unknown (e.g., "Total Goals"), it is voided.

Are my winnings taxable if the bet stands?
